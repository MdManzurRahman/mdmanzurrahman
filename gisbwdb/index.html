    About Us

    Based on the growing need on RS-GIS based planning and management for water resource management in Bangladesh, Central GIS Directorate was established on 14th May, 2019 with the following objectives

    To create, update and store gis based inventory and database for all completated, current and upcoming projects taken by Bangladesh Water Development Board

    River/Coastal Morphology and Flood Risk Mapping including the effect of climate change and suggest/ advise/ recommend /disseminate based on the analysis

    Conduct and Coordinate to develop different numerical (Hydrological, Hydrodynamic, etc.) Models as well as present to and/or receive from concerning consultant/authority
